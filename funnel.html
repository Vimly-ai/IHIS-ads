Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our program

How long is the program?

The program is a total of 13 weeks, 12 weeks of content and instruction plus 1 week to study for and take the final exam.

Is the program entirely online?

Yes, the entire Health & Wellness Coaching Program is online, so you can enroll regardless of where you live.

Do I need prior health and wellness experience?

No! Whether you have a background in health and wellness or not, you can thrive in this program. We teach the fundamentals of health and wellness coaching, which you can take with you to get started in coaching or level up client success by adding on to your existing credentials.

What is included in the curriculum?

Our program was created in alignment with the coaching competencies as listed in the NBHWC Content Outline. It includes coaching structure, coaching process, legal & ethics, scope of practice, coaching psychology, key coaching skills, client goals, collaboration opportunities, the coach approach to health and wellness, Motivational Interviewing, and the Transtheoretical Model of Change (Stages of Change).

Who teaches the program?

Our program includes instruction from faculty with a variety of backgrounds. You'll hear from Board Certified Health & Wellness Coaches, Registered Nurses, Medical Doctors, Family Nurse Practitioners, Naturopathic Doctors, Licensed Professional Counselors and more!

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes! A certificate of completion will be provided upon successfully graduating from the program. This certificate will qualify you to sit for the NBHWC exam if you would like, which upon passing, would give you the designation of Nationally Board Certified Health and Wellness Coach (NBC-HWC).

What are the requirements to sit for the NBHWC board exam?

  • Complete the IHIS Health & Wellness Coaching Program
  • Pass your Practical Skills Assessment (within the program)
  • Complete 50 logged 20+ min coaching sessions
  • Have any associate degree or two years of work experience in any field

For additional information on the board exam, please visit www.nbhwc.org
